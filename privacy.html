Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-05-19

1. Introduction

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Reaction Speed Game (typingspeedgame.my) handles your information.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics, such as page views, device type, and general location. This data helps us improve the website and user experience. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Game Scores: Your game scores and high scores are stored only in your browser (local storage). We do not upload or store your scores on our servers.

3. How We Use Data

  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns (via Google Analytics).
  • To help you track your own game progress (scores stored locally).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

  • You can clear your browser data at any time to remove your local scores.
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using browser add-ons or privacy settings.
